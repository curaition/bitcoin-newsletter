ETH/BTC Breakout According to CryptoPanic's latest observation, the ETH/BTC pair recently broke above its 365-day moving average for the first time since the Ethereum Merge, which demonstrates renewed upward momentum. This breakout follows a strong rebound from the 0.02 support level.
It is important to note that the ETH/BTC ratio had previously hit a high of 0.08, a level that would require a significant price increase from current levels if Bitcoin remains stable against $120,000. The analytics platform added that recent highlights Ethereum's potential for relative strength against Bitcoin if bullish momentum continues in the market.
